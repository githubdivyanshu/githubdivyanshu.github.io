About Me

Hello, my name is Maanas, and I am a dedicated psychologist passionate about helping individuals navigate life's challenges. With over 10 years of experience, my approach is rooted in empathy, evidence-based practices, and a deep respect for each person's unique journey. I specialize in cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) and mindfulness-based interventions to help you build resilience and achieve emotional balance.

My mission is to create a safe and supportive environment where you can explore your thoughts, feelings, and behaviors without judgment. I believe that true wellness is a holistic process that involves mind, body, and spirit. Together, we can work towards a healthier, more fulfilling life.
