Frequently Asked Questions

What can I expect in my first session?

Your first session is an opportunity for us to get to know each other. We will discuss what brings you to therapy, your history, and your goals. It's a collaborative conversation to determine the best path forward and to ensure you feel comfortable with your therapist.

How long does a typical therapy session last?

A standard therapy session lasts for 50 minutes. The frequency of sessions will be determined collaboratively between you and your therapist, but most clients begin with weekly sessions.

Is everything I say confidential?

Yes, confidentiality is a cornerstone of therapy and is protected by law. Everything you discuss is kept private, with a few rare exceptions related to safety (such as risk of harm to yourself or others), which we will discuss with you during your first session.

Do you offer online or remote sessions?

Yes, we offer both in-person sessions at our Jabalpur center and secure online video sessions (telehealth) to accommodate your needs and ensure continuity of care.
